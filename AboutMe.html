I'm Arianna Galimberti.

I'm a Graphic Designer aspirant born in Vimercate, a small town near Monza.

Graduated from Scientific studies, I started my university career studying Interior Design at Politecnico of Milan, but then, after a year, I switched to Communication Design.

I'm obsessed with indie music, bluberries, pink and minimalism and in my free time I love drawing and making doodles. I love taking pictures with my Nikon, particularly of nature and cities. Photography is a kind of relief for me.

Keepin' it real since 1995.
